The Fencly 15-Year Limited Warranty

Fencly warrants its WPC composite fence panels and components against splitting, cracking, rotting, peeling and termite damage for 15 years from the original date of purchase. Issued by Fencly Australia (ABN 69 554 363 962), Wetherill Park NSW.

I. What this warranty covers

Fencly warrants its WPC composite fence products to be free from the following defects for a period of 15 years from the original date of purchase:

  • Splitting of boards or components.
  • Cracking of the WPC material.
  • Rotting of the composite core or surface.
  • Peeling of the outer layer.
  • Termite damage to the WPC core.

Should the product suffer from any of these defects during the warranty period, Fencly will arrange for replacement of any faulty boards or components at no charge to the customer, subject to the conditions outlined below.

II. What this warranty does not cover

  • Damage resulting from accident, misuse, negligence, fire, flood, or other acts of nature.
  • Normal wear and tear, including minor surface weathering.
  • Slight colour fading, which is a natural characteristic of all outdoor products.
  • Damage caused by improper installation or failure to follow Fencly installation guidelines.
  • Damage caused by the use of incompatible components or third-party products.
  • Damage occurring during transport, unloading or handling after delivery.

III. How to make a warranty claim

To make a warranty claim, the customer must contact Fencly Australia and provide the following:

  • Proof of purchase (invoice or order confirmation).
  • Photos clearly showing the defect or damage.
  • Description of the issue and the date it was first noticed.

Fencly will assess the claim within 5 business days of receiving all required documentation. Approved claims will be fulfilled by replacement of defective components. Delivery and labour costs associated with warranty replacements are at the customer's expense unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Submit claims to hello@fencly.com.au or phone +61 421 201 613.

IV. General conditions

This warranty is non-transferable and applies to the original purchaser only. This warranty does not affect your rights under the Australian Consumer Law. Fencly reserves the right to inspect any products subject to a warranty claim prior to approval.
