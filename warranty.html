I

What this warranty covers

Fencly warrants its WPC composite fence products to be free from the following defects for 15 years from the original date of purchase:

  • ✓
    Splitting of boards or components
  • ✓
    Cracking of the WPC material
  • ✓
    Rotting of the composite core or surface
  • ✓
    Peeling of the outer polymer layer
  • ✓
    Termite damage to the WPC core

If a covered defect occurs during the warranty period, Fencly arranges replacement of the faulty boards or components at no charge, subject to the conditions in Sections II and IV.

II

What this warranty does not cover

  • ✕
    Damage from accident, misuse, negligence, fire, flood, or other acts of nature.
  • ✕
    Normal wear and tear, including minor surface weathering.
  • ✕
    Slight colour fading, which is a natural characteristic of all outdoor products.
  • ✕
    Damage caused by improper installation or failure to follow Fencly installation guidelines.
  • ✕
    Damage caused by the use of incompatible components or third-party products.
  • ✕
    Damage occurring during transport, unloading or handling after delivery.
