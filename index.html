Why Co-Extrusion

One layer that means it never rots, fades or splits.

Standard WPC fences absorb water and fade within five summers. Fencly bonds a sealed PE polymer skin around the entire WPC core during manufacture — a 360° suit of armour that UV, salt-air and stains can't get past.

  1. 01

    UV-stable surface

    Pigment is locked into the polymer skin during co-extrusion — no surface coating to chip or chalk. Tested for 5,000+ hours under accelerated AU sun.

  2. 02

    360° PE co-ex cap

    The sealed polyethylene skin wraps every face — top, bottom and both sides — so water, salt-air and dirt never reach the wood fibre. Standard WPC only caps three sides.

  3. 03

    Molecular bond

    Cap and core are extruded simultaneously and fused at 195°C. There is no glue line to peel — the two layers are chemically welded into one board.

  4. 04

    Dense WPC core

    Recycled hardwood fibres compressed with HDPE polymer. Twice the density of standard WPC — the reason Fencly boards span 1.83m without sag.
